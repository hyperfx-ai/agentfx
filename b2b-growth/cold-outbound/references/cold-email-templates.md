# Cold Email Templates

## Template Instructions

1. Replace `[THEIR_NAME]`, `[THEIR_COMPANY]`, `[YOUR_NAME]`, `[YOUR_PRODUCT]`
2. Keep subject lines under 50 characters
3. Keep body under 100 words
4. One CTA per email
5. Send from personal email (not marketing@)

---

## Template 1: The Zero-Click (Awareness Only)

**Subject:** Quick intro - [YOUR_PRODUCT]

**Body:**
```
Hi [THEIR_NAME],

Saw you're [THEIR_ROLE] at [THEIR_COMPANY].

We built [YOUR_PRODUCT] - it helps [SPECIFIC ROLE] with [SPECIFIC PROBLEM].

A few companies like [SIMILAR_COMPANY] have been using it to [SPECIFIC_RESULT].

Figured it might be relevant.

[YOUR_NAME]
```

**Use when:** You have self-service product, just want awareness

## Sending Strategy

**Volume:**
- Start with 100/day per sending domain
- Warm up for 2 weeks before scaling
- Max 5,000/month per email address

**Segmentation:**
- Different templates for different personas
- A/B test subject lines
- Track reply rate by template

**Follow-up Sequence:**
- Email 1: Day 0
- Email 2: Day 4 (if no reply)
- Email 3: Day 10 (if no reply)
- Email 4: Day 20 (break-up email)

**Success Metrics:**
- 1-2% reply rate = good
- 3-5% reply rate = great
- 5%+ reply rate = exceptional
