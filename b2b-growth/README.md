Proven B2B SaaS growth tactics with copy-paste templates across paid, outbound, and organic channels.

## Overview

These skills provide battle-tested templates and frameworks for B2B SaaS growth. Built from real campaigns that have generated millions in revenue.

Cold email templates and LinkedIn outreach sequences. 15+ email templates and multi-touch sequences that get responses and book meetings.

**Channels**: Email, LinkedIn DM

Templates for organic audience building through podcasts, newsletters, and YouTube. Focus on long-term brand building.

**When to use**: Building thought leadership, creating content flywheel

**Channels**: Podcasts (guest), Newsletter, YouTube

## Quick Start Guide

### For Immediate Revenue (0-90 days)

**Week 1-2: Paid Ads**
1. Use [paid-ads](./paid-ads) skill
2. Set up Facebook + Google campaigns
3. Budget: $3k-5k/month minimum
4. Test 3-5 ad variations per channel

**Week 1-4: Cold Outbound**
1. Use [cold-outbound](./cold-outbound) skill
2. Build list of 500 prospects
3. Launch email sequences
4. Expect: 5-10 meetings booked

**Expected Result**: First customers within 30-60 days

### For Long-Term Growth (6-12 months)

**Month 1-3: Start Organic**
1. Use [organic-content](./organic-content) skill  
2. Launch weekly newsletter
3. Book 3-5 podcast appearances
4. Start YouTube channel (optional)

**Month 4-6: Scale & Iterate**
1. Double down on working paid channels
2. Expand outbound to new segments
3. Continue organic consistency

**Month 7-12: Compound**
1. Organic content starts driving inbound
2. Paid acquisition costs decrease (brand awareness)
3. Outbound response rates improve (warm audience)

**Expected Result**: Sustainable growth engine by Month 12

## Recommended Channel Mix

### Early Stage (<$1M ARR)

**70% Outbound** - Founder-led, high-touch
- Most predictable and controllable
- Skills: cold-outbound

**20% Paid** - Test and learn
- Validate messaging and channels
- Skills: paid-ads

**10% Organic** - Plant seeds
- Won't pay off yet, but start building
- Skills: organic-content

### Growth Stage ($1-10M ARR)

**40% Paid** - Scale what works
- Predictable customer acquisition
- Skills: paid-ads

**30% Outbound** - Sales team execution
- Outbound SDR team
- Skills: cold-outbound (scaled)

**30% Organic** - Inbound engine
- Content flywheel starting to work
- Skills: organic-content

### Scale Stage ($10M+ ARR)

**50% Organic** - Dominant channel
- Inbound leads at scale
- Skills: organic-content (primary)

**30% Paid** - Fill pipeline gaps
- Always-on campaigns
- Skills: paid-ads (optimization)

**20% Outbound** - Enterprise deals
- Strategic account targeting
- Skills: cold-outbound (high-touch)

## Core Principles Across All Channels

### 1. Value First

**The Pattern:**
Value → Value → Value → Value → Value → Value → Ask

Give before you ask. Applies to ads, emails, content.

### 2. Test Everything

- Test 3-5 variations minimum
- Give it 7-14 days minimum
- Track metrics rigorously
- Kill losers, scale winners

### 3. Consistency Compounds

**Paid**: Algorithms reward consistency
**Outbound**: Multi-touch wins
**Organic**: Weekly shipping beats monthly perfection

### 4. One Thing at a Time

Change one variable per test:
- Hook OR pain OR solution
- Subject line OR body OR CTA
- Not all at once

## Performance Benchmarks

### Paid Ads
- **Facebook**: $20-100 CPA, 1-3% CTR
- **Google**: $50-200 CPA, 3-7% CTR  
- **LinkedIn**: $100-300 CPA, 0.5-1% CTR

### Cold Outbound
- **Email**: 1-5% response rate
- **LinkedIn**: 5-15% response rate
- **Meetings**: 0.5-2% of outreach converts to meeting

### Organic
- **Newsletter**: 40-60% open rate, 10-50 new subs/week
- **Podcast**: 50-500 site visitors per appearance
- **YouTube**: 5-10% CTR, compounds over time

## Budget Recommendations

### Minimum Viable Budget

**Paid Ads**: $3k-5k/month
- Split: Facebook $2k, Google $2k, LinkedIn $1k
- Test phase: 3 months minimum

**Outbound Tools**: $200-500/month
- Email tools, LinkedIn premium, CRM
- Manual effort: 10-20 hours/week

**Organic**: $0 (pure time investment)
- 5-10 hours/week
- Consistency for 6-12 months

### Scaling Budget ($10k+/month growth budget)

**Paid**: $7k/month
**Outbound**: $2k/month (tools + VA)
**Organic**: $1k/month (editing, design help)

## Common Mistakes Across All Channels

❌ **Starting too broad** - Pick ONE channel, go deep
❌ **Quitting too early** - Need 90 days minimum
❌ **Not tracking metrics** - Can't improve what you don't measure
❌ **Too promotional** - Value first, always
❌ **Inconsistency** - Kills momentum in all channels
❌ **No testing** - One variation = you're guessing
