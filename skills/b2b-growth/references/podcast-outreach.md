## Production Schedule

**Weekly Workflow (90 min total):**

Monday (30 min):
- Send 3-5 guest outreach emails
- Review responses
- Schedule recordings

Tuesday (60 min):
- Record 1 podcast episode
- Send thank you email to guest

Wednesday (15 min):
- Extract 10 insights with Claude
- Write newsletter

Thursday (15 min):
- Create 5-7 social clips
- Schedule posts

Friday (15 min):
- Monitor engagement
- DM people who commented
- Schedule next guests

**Content Output:**
- 1 full podcast episode
- 1 email newsletter
- 7 social clips
- 10 quote graphics
- 1 guest relationship

**Distribution:**
- Apple Podcasts
- Spotify
- YouTube (video)
- Newsletter (email list)
- LinkedIn (clips)
- Twitter (clips)

**Promotion:**
- Guest shares to their audience
- You share to yours
- Email list gets first access
- Social clips throughout week
