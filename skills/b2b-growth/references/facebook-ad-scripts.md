# Facebook Ad Scripts (UGC Format)

All scripts follow: **Hook (3s) → Pain (7s) → Solution (15s)**

## Template Instructions

1. Replace `[PRODUCT]` with your product name
2. Replace `[PAIN POINT]` with specific customer pain
3. Replace `[OUTCOME]` with the result they get
4. Keep under 30 seconds total
5. Shoot in selfie-style video (authentic, not polished)

---

## Production Notes

**Visual Style:**
- Selfie-style, good lighting
- Show the product on screen when mentioning it
- Be authentic, not salesy
- Talk like a human, not a commercial

**Posting Strategy:**
- Test all 10 scripts with $50-100 each
- Run for 3-7 days
- Find 2-3 with lowest CPC
- Create new conversion campaigns with winners
- Scale budget on winners only
